In my thesis I explore a some of the ways that after 9/11 the US government cultivated and perpetuated a national climate of fear and how that has lead zombies to huge popularity in America. I believe one of the methods used to cultivate this national climate of fear was leaders and legislators successfully changing the language used to talk about immigration. Before 9/11 the language used to talk about immigration described an America open to diversity and inclusion. Post-9/11 the language used to talk about immigration described an America needing to protect itself from anyone trying to enter the country legally or illegally as a possible security threat.

With this project I wanted to explore a possible connection between key words recorded in the congressional record for specific years and the output of American zombie films for those same years. The key words I searched for are "immigration and safety" in any single recorded instance. I have searched 5 years pre 9/11 and 5 years post 9/11.
